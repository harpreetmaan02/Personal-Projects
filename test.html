Sales Analysis Project

Project Overview

In this comprehensive sales analysis project, we aimed to leverage the power of Power BI and implement a star schema for efficient data representation. The project involved the integration of six vital datasets: dates, sales, stores, salespeople, products, and a sales query. The primary goal was to delve into sales trends, product performance, and customer segments to provide actionable insights for decision-making.

Project Objectives

  • Implement a star schema in Power BI for structured and efficient sales analysis.
  • Define and organize dimensions and a central fact table to unlock valuable insights.
  • Load data through Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) processes for data integrity and consistency.
  • Create queries for analyzing sales trends, product performance, and customer segments.
  • Empower decision-makers with actionable insights to optimize processes and drive business success.

Modelling & Results

The heart of the project lies in the star schema, providing a structured approach to organizing dimensions (dates, sales, stores, salespeople, products) around a central fact table (sales query). This design allows for efficient analysis, enabling businesses to navigate through complex datasets seamlessly.

The results obtained from this approach empower decision-makers to make informed choices, optimize processes, and drive overall business success. As we continue to navigate the data-driven landscape, adopting such methodologies becomes imperative for staying competitive and agile in today's dynamic business environment.
