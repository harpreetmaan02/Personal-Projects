Zomato EDA Report

Project Overview

In this exploratory data analysis (EDA) project for Zomato, we aimed to analyze data from over 100,000 restaurants nationwide. The project involved strategic data analysis for targeted marketing campaigns, substantial year-over-year revenue growth, and customized data-driven solutions for optimizing inventory management.

Project Objectives

  • Enhance lead generation by 15% through strategic analysis of data for targeted marketing campaigns.
  • Drive substantial year-over-year revenue growth with data-driven sales strategies.
  • Provide customized data-driven solutions for optimizing inventory management.

Results

The analysis resulted in enhanced lead generation, substantial revenue growth, and optimized inventory management. These data-driven insights empower decision-makers to make informed choices, optimize processes, and drive overall business success in the competitive restaurant industry.
